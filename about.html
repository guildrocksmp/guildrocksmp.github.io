About

What is this SMP?

➤ [CCC] GuildRock SMP is a Minecraft bedrock server dedicated for devoted, professional players, content and non-content creators alike, with splendid building skills and/or superb technical skills who are looking to collaborate and peacefully compete with others who possess similar abilities in an economy-based (HermitCraft-like) environment.

What do the members do?

➤ With seasons that last anywhere from 1-2 years, the members aim to construct monumental bases, engineer outrageous farms, participate in the economy by building shops and mini games, as well as collaborate with each other to ultimately have a fantastic time in this tight-knit community.

Is the SMP vanilla?

➤ The SMP is semi-vanilla as we make use of carefully selected behavior packs that optimize the game by providing quality-of-life changes and fixing some of the illogical logistics of the game; however, these are not game breaking modifications, so the members can feel as though they are playing on a completely vanilla world.
